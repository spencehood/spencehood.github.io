    Welcome to the CU Buffoons Setlist App.

    Select which singers will be missing from the upcoming show, and see which songs you'll be able to sing based on soloist, beatboxer, etc. Use the additional factors next to the singers to filter your search even further.

    • Pink tiles indicate that the soloist will be missing, but the understudy will be available.
    • Blue tiles indicate that both the beatboxer and backup beatboxer will be missing, so someone new will have to take over beatboxing.
    • Click each tile to see that song's full information in the lower right corner of the page.

    This warning signifies that you've got a whole vocal part missing, which ain't no good.
